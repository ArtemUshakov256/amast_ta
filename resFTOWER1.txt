Key for symbols:
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ty    = Member type
        1 = Leg or chord member (lower/outer)
        2 = Chord member (upper/inner)
        3 = Diagonal member (front face)
        4 = Diagonal member (side/lower/outer face)
        5 = Diagonal member (inner/upper face)
        6 = Horizontal member (front face)
        7 = Horizontal member (side/lower/outer face)
        8 = Horizontal member (inner/upper face or middle)
        9 = Stiffener member
       10 = Redundant member
       11 = Plan frame member
G     = Member group nr in sub-structure
Leng  = Total length of member (mm)
Lbuc  = Buckling length of member (- sign means buckling about x-x axis)
Sle   = Effective slenderness (incl. end restraint effects)
Fbuc  = Design force for buckling (N)
Lc    = Nr of load case controlling the buckling
N     = Nr of special item for buckling of bracings (see separate figure)
        1 = Stability of 1 memb due to v-v axis for length L1 with Fbuc=Fmin
        2 = Stability of 1 memb due to x-x axis for length L2 with Fbuc=Fmin
        3 = Stability of 2 memb in same face due to x-x axis for length L3 with Fbuc=Fave
        4 = Stability of 2 memb in adj faces due to x-x axis for length L4 with Fbuc=Fave
        5 = Stability of 4 memb due to x-x axis for length L5 with Fbuc=Fave
            Fave = Average member force of participating members in case concerned
Fmin  = Minimum force (N), i.e. max compression
Lc    = Nr of load case giving the minimum force
Fmax  = Maximum force (N), i.e. max tension
Lc    = Nr of load case giving the maximum force
Cbuc  = Maximum use factor for buckling
Cten  = Maximum use factor for tension
Cbea  = Maximum use factor for bearing at bolt hole
Cbol  = Maximum use factor for bolt shear or tension
Cben  = Maximum use factor for bending (manload)
Cmax  = Maximum use factor of all above
Mark at the end of line (if any):
 *    = Allowable load capacity exceeded (i.e. any of Ci>1)
 +    = Allowable slenderness exceeded
